Teledentistry Consultation Services


Schedule your online consultation today!

Due to the recent pandemic a lot of dental offices have had to close their doors or can see emergency patients only.  If you have a dental emergency or a dental question we are offering dental consultations using secure HIPAA-compliant web-based software. These online face to face consultations will allow us to perform a limited evaluation in order to determine if you should be seen now, or if a visit is better to be scheduled in the future.

These services are covered by most dental insurances. Click in the Calendar to schedule your online dental consultation with Dr Cutler and our Dental team. You are not alone, we are here to help.
