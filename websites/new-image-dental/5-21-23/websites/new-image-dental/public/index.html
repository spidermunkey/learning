Emergency?

Dental emergencies are not only painful; they can also be frightening. In most cases, prompt treatment is required to alleviate pain and ensure the patient's oral and overall health.

If you're dealing with a dental emergency, call us immediately at (307) 222-1002, and we will schedule you in as soon as possible for an emergency dental treatment in Cheyenne, WY.
