 COVID-19

Message to our patients and friends:

 We are following all the latest recommendations from CDC in response to COVIT-19 outbreak. We want to assure you that your safety is our first priority. Infection control procedures are actions taken in health care settings to prevent the spread of disease everyday. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention has recommendations for dental office infection control. Here at New Image Dental we care about your safety and work hard to prevent the spread of infection. Before you enter any of our examining rooms or reception areas, all surfaces, such as the dental chair, dental light, drawer handles, countertops and reception area furniture, has been cleaned and disinfected. We also cover some equipment with protective covers, which are replaced after each patient.
