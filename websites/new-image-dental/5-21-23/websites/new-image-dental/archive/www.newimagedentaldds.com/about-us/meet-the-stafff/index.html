About New Image Dental

At New Image Dental, your smile and oral health are our top priorities. Our entire team is dedicated to providing you with the personalized, gentle care that you and your family deserve. That is why we offer the finest in dental services by using state-of-the-art materials and technology in the area of 3D Pano imaging, same day crowns, and the latest in laser technology.

We have been offering Quality Dental Care for Over 25 Years﻿

For nearly three decades, our friendly dentist—Richard S. Cutler DDS—has provided high-quality dental services to patients of all ages. Our professional staff members go out of their way to make every patient feel comfortable during their appointment. As a family dentist, Dr. Cutler stays up-to-date with everything in the ever-changing world of dentistry. Whether you need a root canal, filling, or teeth whitening services, our dental staff has the knowledge and skills to make your treatment a positive experience and keep you smiling for years to come.

Meet the New Image Dental Staff

Here at New Image Dental, we're known for bringing a new level of care to the dentistry field. For more than 25 years, people have trusted our team for a wide range of cosmetic and general dentistry services. Patients of all ages appreciate our friendly attitude, relaxed atmosphere, and smiling faces. When you meet our staff, you'll immediately feel at home. Each member of our team goes above and beyond to make sure you have a comfortable and pleasant dental experience.

 Our Dentist, Richard S. Cutler, DDS

Dr. Cutler is a native of the Chicago area and a graduate of Northwestern Dental School. Before heading west to Cheyenne, Dr. Cutler had a thriving private practice in downtown Chicago. His desire to live in the west came from many summer visits to the area as a child with his family.


Dr. Cutler has been practicing dentistry for well over 25 years, including eight years in Cheyenne. He has traveled to most of the world's continents and has helped and trained many people in third-world countries concerning dental and oral health. Dr. Cutler's goal is to not only give you a great smile but to provide you with exceptional service from the moment you call our office. Reach out to us today to experience it for yourself.
